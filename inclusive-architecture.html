⚖️ Inclusive AI Architecture

Consequences of Exclusion

  • 🚫 Barriers instead of supports - AI designed for neurotypical cognition
  • 🎭 Bias disguised as objectivity - AI misreads neurodivergent behavior
  • 💼 Workplace bias in recruitment - Algorithms filter out neurodivergent candidates
  • 💡 Missed innovation - Neurodivergent thinkers spot cracks before others
  • 📜 Legal/ethical blind spots - Violates UN Convention on Rights

Practical Strategies for Inclusion

🤝 Co-design - Involve neurodivergent people at every stage
🔍 Neurodivergent-led audits - Independent teams stress-test systems
🔄 Flip mentorship - Pair leaders with neurodivergent mentors
📖 Explain black box - Plain-language explanations for AI decisions
💰 Fund different labs - Invest in neurodivergent innovation labs
🔄 Continuous feedback - Long-term advisory councils
📊 Improve data inside - Neurodivergent people build datasets
👥 Diversify oversight - Regulatory bodies reflect diverse patterns
